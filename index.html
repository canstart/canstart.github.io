Canon Ij Setup

Canon.com/ijsetup



Canon.com/ijsetup is the official web portal provided by Canon for setting up and configuring Canon inkjet printers. When you purchase a new Canon printer, you’ll need to follow specific steps to get it ready for use. These steps include unboxing, connecting to power, loading ink cartridges, connecting to Wi-Fi, and installing the necessary software. Canon.com/ijsetup serves as a central hub where users can find detailed instructions, download drivers, and troubleshoot any issues related to their Canon inkjet printers.


Get Started with Canon Ij Setup


  • Visit Canon.com/ijsetup and enter your printer model number.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions and download any necessary software or drivers.
  • Connect your printer to your computer or network.
  • Complete the setup process outlined in the guided instructions.

Download Canon Printer Drivers


  • First, go to Canon.com/ijsetup official website.
  • Next, select the content you wish to download. And click the Drivers option.
  • Now, click the drop-down arrow and select the operating system.
  • Afterward, click on the Download button.
  • Next, accept the software license agreement.
  • Tap on the Save File button now and run the driver download.

Steps to Install Canon Drivers via Canon.com/ijsetup


  • Double-click on the “Canon driver package” file on your device.
  • Next, reach the welcome screen. And identify the option Start Setup.
  • Click on it to initiate the installation of the driver.
  • Soon, you’ll see a message on the screen that the installation steps have been completed.
  • Proceed to subsequently click on the region, country, and Next button.
  • Now, click on the Yes option after accepting the license agreement.
  • Notice and select the option titled Agree.
  • Next, click on the Next button.
  • Afterward, select the option USB Connection and click on the Next button.
  • Please wait till the installer installs the drivers on your device.
  • After that, connect your Canon printer and your device via USB cable and press the power button present on the Control panel.
  • Finally, finish the process of installation and try printing.

Canon Printer IJ Setup Using the USB Cable


  • If you want to complete the Canon.com/ijsetup through a wired connection then you need to choose the USB option when prompted to select the network connection.
  • Now you will see on the screen where you need to jump on the USB connection option on the computer. If you are a Mac user click on the next option.
  • Now, you will see on the screen the access points that are connecting the computer. If you want the printer to connect to that network, click on the yes option.
  • In the case of Mac, click on the allow option. Now on the screen, you need to dismantle your printer from the computer and then click on allow.
  • In the case of Windows, click on ok.
  • Then click on the add printer option.
  • Then select the particular driver and click on add.
  • Once the Canon Ij Setup is complete click on Next.
